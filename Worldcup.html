📋 How It Works

1
Group Stage: Predict the exact score for all 72 group matches across 12 groups
2
Auto-Standings: The system calculates group tables from your predictions — top 2 + best 8 third-place teams advance
3
Third-Place Picks: Rank all 12 third-place teams — your top 8 are your qualifier picks
4
Knockout Rounds: Pick winners for all 32 knockout matches (Round of 32 → Final)
5
Champion Pick: Predict who wins the World Cup (worth 175 points!)
6
Tiebreaker: Predict the exact Final score — used only to break ties on points
